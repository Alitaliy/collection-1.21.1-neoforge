# Collection 0.5.1

`0.5.1` is a small gameplay fix for the new Collector villager loop.

## Trading

- Novice Collectors still sell the Collector's Journal.
- Novice Collectors now also offer one random collectible recycling trade.
- The recycling trade lets players sell 1 collectible item for 3 emeralds.
- Recycling collectibles grants Collector villager XP, so players can level a Collector without repeatedly buying duplicate journals.
