# Collection 0.5.0

`0.5.0` adds the Collector villager loop and moves clue maps into villager trading.

## New Content

- Added the Collector villager profession.
- Added the Collector's Workstation job-site block.
- Added a unified Collector house for vanilla villages.
- Added Collector houses to plains, desert, savanna, snowy, and taiga village house pools.

## Trading

- Novice Collectors sell the Collector's Journal.
- Apprentice and higher Collectors sell clue maps for collectible sets.
- Higher villager levels unlock more clue categories, ending with Shrine Effigy clue maps at master level.

## Gameplay Changes

- Sneak-using the Collector's Journal no longer gives a free clue map.
- Clue maps now come from Collector villager trades.
